# Product

## Register

brand

## Creative North Star

The Digital Space Agency

## Users

Local Indian business owners (Ankleshwar, Bareilly, and surrounding regions) who need modern digital presence — websites, automation, chatbots, SaaS. They are time-pressed, value-conscious, and want to compete with bigger brands. They trust local providers who demonstrate technical authority.

Secondary: national/smb founders evaluating the agency's quality through the website itself.

## Product Purpose

KritRaj Nexera sells digital development services via a premium portfolio website. The site itself is the strongest proof of capability — it must look, feel, and perform like a $50k agency build. Every section must convert: educate → build trust → drive contact.

## Brand Personality

Futuristic, bold, trustworthy, next-gen. Young founder energy paired with enterprise-level craft. The brand is confident enough to use restraint — one or two accent colors, generous whitespace, editorial typography. It says "we know what we're doing" without shouting.

## Anti-references

- Generic dark SaaS template (purple blob gradients, three-card icon grids, glassmorphism)
- Stock rocket/laptop illustrations
- Bouncing animations or cheap motion
- Pure black backgrounds (use #07091A tinted deep navy instead)
- Inter-only typography
- "AI-made" tells: numbered section markers on every section, tiny uppercase eyebrows above every heading, identical card grids

## Design Principles

1. **AI-accelerated craftsmanship** — speed without sacrifice. Use AI tools to deliver faster, but hand-finish every detail.
2. **Engineering-led aesthetic** — code as visual language. Mono labels, hairline borders, terminal-like data displays.
3. **Dark-spatial depth** — ink space as canvas. The background is not black; it's a deep, tinted navy that creates atmosphere.
4. **Restrained accent** — violet + cyan used surgically as signals, not decoration. One accent moment per viewport.
5. **Local-first narrative** — built for Indian businesses. City names, price points in INR, WhatsApp as primary contact channel.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum for body text contrast
- Reduced motion honored via prefers-reduced-motion — all animations degrade gracefully to opacity-only
- Focus-visible outlines on all interactive elements
- Target sizes ≥ 44×44px on touch
- No information conveyed by color alone
